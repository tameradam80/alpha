Areas of Expertise

Events Management

Among our various areas of expertise is events management, where we deliver dynamic content that boosts engagement and interaction levels between the sponsors and the attendees of virtual, hybrid, and in-person events. Furthermore, we offer a digital platform that shares updates about live and upcoming sessions, access to a 24/7 customer support team trained to respond to event-specific queries, and web analytics on customer feedback.
When it comes to addressing the needs of attendees joining the event virtually, we use our advanced video conferencing technology to share safe and high quality live streams, video sessions on demand with interactive tools such as audience questions and answers and build virtual appointments and collaboration sessions within the community. Additionally, we allow the attendees to stay connected through their browser or mobile device with the native event app –before, during and after the event.
As for the participants and sponsors of the virtual event, we offer multiple channels that aid specifically in comparing virtual event data with personal and hybrid events for a complete picture of the buyer's interest as well as tracking how participants interact with our exhibitor’s virtual booths, video rooms, and downloadable content to calculate engagement scores that could translate to potential sales.

Digital Marketing & PR Solutions

Through the intertwining of digital marketing and public relations, we help business owners drive traffic towards their social media and webpage by boosting their SEO rankings, making more people aware of their current and upcoming events and building brand trust through online reviews. We use common metric systems and analytical tools to measure awareness, engagement and conversion levels, so organizers can have a good idea of how many eyes are seeing their ad placements and the purchases or registrations made from particular placements.

Artificial Intelligence

Another way we can use AI is by creating chatbots, where these bots help answer frequently asked questions, solve problems, suggest products or services, and support sales. AI also has the potential of transforming human interaction-based activities through machine-learning and algorithms, thus improving supply chain management by automating repetitive tasks.
